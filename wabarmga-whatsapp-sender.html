WABarmga - Whatsapp Sender

Send mass whatsapp messages hands free!
WABarmga, a browser extension that automates sending bulk WhatsApp messages from a list of phone numbers.

  Download    How It Works 
  • 1.

    Install the Extension

    Install the WABarmga extension on desktop chrome browser, from Chrome web store.

  • 2.

    Open web.whatsapp.com

    Go to web.whatsapp.com and sign in to your account.
    If not signed in already, open whatsapp mobile application, click on top left three points button, go to linked devices, click link device and take a photo of the QR code.

  • 3.

    Click on WABarmga Button

    Click the WABarmga button located on the top left of your screen.
    This button only shows up on Whatsapp web page.

  • 4.

    Enter Message & Start

    Enter your message, phone numbers, and time delay, then click start.
    Use close minimum and maximum delays like: 100 & 150 seconds.
    You can download phone numbers databases from the potential customer data collection.
