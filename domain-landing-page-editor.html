Generate Google Form Link

First, go to mail.google.com and ensure you are signed in with your Gmail account.

  • Step 1: Make a copy of the form.

    Go to this link: Google Drive Folder. Click the three dots on the form file and select Make a copy.

  • Step 2: Find the copied form.

    Go to drive.google.com/drive and double click on the copied form to open it. Usually, you will find it under the name: Copy of Domain Sales Report. You can also open it through the 3 dots menu as displayed in the image.

  • Step 3: Enable responses.

    At the top of the form, click Responses and make sure that Accepting responses is enabled.

  • Step 4: Link Google Sheet.

    Click the Responses button. Click on Link to Sheets then create button. Copy & save the sheet link, from the URL bar.

  • Step 5: Copy the shareable link.

    Click the Share button, then make sure the link is public. Click on Copy Responder Link.

  • Step 6: Paste the link.

    Return to this page and paste the link you just copied into the Google Form Link input field.
